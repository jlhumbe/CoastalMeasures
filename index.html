  • DriftAlong is a small passive drifter for use in coastal regions such as estuaries and the nearshore ocean. A simple GUI allows users to start/stop collections and transfer data to their computers. Position information, including measures of uncertainty, are recorded at 1Hz. Base models of DriftAlong are available starting at $300 and upgraded versions with wireless communication capabilities and remote data downloads (DriftAlong-W) are available starting at $450. With DriftAlong-W's, individual units or 'swarms' of units may be communicated with in real time from an online dashboard to initiate or end collections or retrieve current positions and statuses.
