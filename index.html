• DriftAlong is a small passive drifter for use in coastal or inland waters. Collections may be begun or ended via a GUI on a small touch screen on the instrument, or remotely from a computer or smartphone. An online dashboard allows users to communicate with DriftAlongs in mass to simplify 'swarm' deployments. Units start at $499 per unit without a remote connection and at $699 with a remote connection. Please contact us to learn more.

  • Coastal communities know all too well that strong weather systems often bring with them dangerous and damaging flooding However, it remains difficult to prepare for these events without knowing when or how severe this flooding will be. The Coastal Flood Alert System (CFAS) is a hardware-software-service package using water level sensors and a custom scientific analysis to provide locally catered short-term (1-3days) flood predictions based on the unique environmental setup of each community. With test cases improving water level predictions by as much as 40%, CFAS will help residents prepare or evacuate earlier and provide much improved guidance for emergency response teams.
