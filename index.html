  • DriftAlong is a small passive drifter for use in coastal regions such as estuaries and the nerashore ocean. A simple GUI allows users to start/stop collections and transfer data to their computers. Position information, including measures of uncertainty, are recorded at 1Hz. Base models of DriftAlong are available starting at $300 and upgraded versions with wireless communication capabilities and remote data downloads (DriftAlong-W) are available starting at $450. With DriftAlong-W's, individual units or 'swarms' of units may be communicated with in real time from an online dashboard where collections may be begun or ended. Units or swarms may also be queried for their current position and status. Please contact us to learn more.
